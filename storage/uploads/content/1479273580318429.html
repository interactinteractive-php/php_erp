Generally speaking, you can start any project on any day. When you apply, you will specify the start and end dates. Our projects run year round and have volunteers arriving almost every day.

zoom

Since our projects sometimes depend on the local holiday calendar, there are brief occasions when normal project work is suspended. For example, schools often close for long winter/summer holidays. During these times, we arrange for alternative work projects that are similar in substance to our ongoing projects, but address the short-term, direct needs of the local communities. For example, during the summer holiday (January – early March) on our Peru Teaching program, volunteers run a teacher training course for local ESL instructors.

If you would like to arrange your start and end dates around the local holidays, please call us at 1.888.839.3535 or email us at info@projects-abroad.org. In addition, you will also have the opportunity, after you are accepted into the program, to speak with a staff member about local holidays.
